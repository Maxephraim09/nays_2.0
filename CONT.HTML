Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I become a member?

Click the "Register" button in the top right corner and complete the registration form. You'll need to provide your educational information and upload required documents.

What are the membership fees?

Secondary: ₦1,000/year | Tertiary: ₦2,000/year | Alumni: ₦5,000/year (or ₦50,000 lifetime) | Diaspora: $20/year

How can I donate to projects?

Visit our Projects section, choose a project you'd like to support, and click the "Donate" button. You can pay via card, bank transfer, or USSD.

How do I access NAYS Academy?

Once logged in, click on "Academy" in the navigation menu. You'll have access to practice exams, JAMB/WAEC preparation materials, and leaderboards.
